Key Features:
-   **Multi-Role Authentication:** JWT-based auth for Player, Venue Owner, Coach, and Super Admin.

-   **SaaS Subscriptions:** Enforces plan-based limits for Venue Owners.
-   **Mercenary Marketplace:** Players can hire fill-in players for their booked games, with an integrated payment flow.

-   **Tamper-Proof Rating System:** Player rating changes are recorded in a verifiable, hash-chained log to prevent tampering and build trust. Each player has a Rating Profile page with a certificate of integrity.
-   **Notifications:** An in-app notification system alerts users to key events.
